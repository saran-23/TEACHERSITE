SBAOC

Time : Three hours
Marks : 75


Section-A
Answer any TEN questions

1. What is PCI?

2. What are the two steps involved in instruction processing?

3. What is the purpose of Cache memory?

4. What are the two forms of RAM used in computers?

5. List any two applications of ROM.

6. What are the advantages of using a glass substrate for a magnetic disk?

7. What is the International Reference Alphabet?

8. Name the two common types of I/O channel.

9. What is PSW?

10. What is pipelining?

11. What is microprogram?

12. What is microprammed control unit?


Section-B
Answer any FIVE questions

13. What are elements of bus design? Explain.

14. Write a short note on memory hierarchy.

15. Explain briefly the types of ROM.

16. Explain the format of Winchester Disk.

17. List and briefly explain the five important instruction set design issues.

18. Explain the internal structure of CPU.

19. Explain the ways of determining the next microinstructions address.


Section-C
Answer any THREE questions

20. Describe the key characteristics of memory system in detail.

21. Write a detailed note on magnetic tape.

22. Describe the various addressing mode techniques.
